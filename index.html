The Origin Story

She kept
showing
up.

Jana has always moved differently. Meal prepping, lifting, training — pushing everyone around her to take care of themselves, even while carrying her family on her back.

A year and a half ago she stepped onto the mats. Jiu-jitsu didn't just become a hobby — it became her identity. She went almost every single day, earned her blue belt, and became someone her whole circle looked up to.

Then she put on her gi for the first time, looked into the camera, and said the word that started it all:

"SHOOPA!"

Now she's building the place she always needed: where girls come to fight, think, read, and grow — together.
